About us

A small, family-run house in Abilene, Texas.

We've been raising tiny Shih Tzu for over twenty years. Every puppy is born inside the home, raised inside the home, paper-trained on schedule, and current on every vaccine and worming round before they leave us.

We breed for the cobby, flat-faced, smaller side of the Shih Tzu standard — most of our adults are below the 9-pound show minimum, which is exactly the pocket-sized type we're after. We have a particular love for the rarer chocolate and liver coats: dark chocolate, light chocolate, white chocolate, the whole range.

We are hobby breeders. We show with the AKC for the love of the breed, not for championships. The dogs that don't quite make show conformation are placed as pets — honestly, with full disclosure of why they wouldn't have showed.

Our Promise

What every puppy comes with.

i

Health guaranteed

Free of known genetic defect that would cause death, certified by us at placement and validated by your vet within the contract window.

ii

Vaccines current

Up to date on every round, wormed on schedule. Records travel home with the puppy.

iii

AKC papers

Full AKC or CKC, your choice. Full AKC removes breeding restrictions — we disclose what we observed.

iv

Honest match

If a puppy isn't right for show, we tell you why and place them as pets. We match by fit, not by deposit speed.
